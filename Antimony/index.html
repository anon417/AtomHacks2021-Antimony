About the Issue


Every year, over 8 million tons of plastic ends up in our oceans, poisoning sea life and destroying our planet. This issue is not going to solve itself. There is only one way to solve this problem, and that's by creating awareness throughout the world. Believe it or not, most people are uniformed about the severity of the situation. This means we must get people like YOU to take action. In small steps, we are helping our environment, and leaving a better world for future generations.

How can I help?

Reduce your total plastic usage. Only use plastic when necessary, and try to choose products with less packaging.

Reuse old plastic instead of throwing it away. Instead of buying a new water bottle, refill it with water from home. Avoid disposable items like plastic shopping bags and styrofoam.

Recycle plastic back into the system, so it doesn't need to be thrown away. When purchasing items, try to find things that can be recycled.

About this Website


This website was developed for the 2021 AtomHacks competition. Weight averages were determined based on unit weights for the most common brands as well as experimentally determined weights from various studies. Total plastic calculations are the sum of the total weights of each item.
